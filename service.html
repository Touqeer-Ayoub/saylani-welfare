Services - Health - Saylani Health Department

Saylani Health Department

The Saylani Medical and Diagnostic Center provides quality healthcare facilities in which General O.P.D, Consultant Clinic, Diabetes, Hepatitis Treatment Center, Ophthalmology and O.P.D. It also provides dental services, laboratory tests, physiotherapy, mobile health clinics and oriental clinic facilities including cupping therapy, homeopathy and wisdom.

Services - Sadiqah Jariah - Construction of Masajid

Construction of Masajid

The construction work of mosques in remote areas and new settlements of Karachi city is also carried out by Saylani Welfare International Trust. Hundreds of mosques have been built so far.

Services - Education - Schooling System

Schooling System

Saylani Welfare has been providing primary and secondary education to millions of children. Under this, many children from poor families are becoming an asset to the country through education. Now Saylani has set up a regular academy named Saylani Al – Mohsin Academy has been set up. Under this academy, free educational facilities will be provided to students in accordance with international standards.

Saylani Ehsaas - Koi Bhooka Na Soye Ga

Koi Bhooka Na Soye Ga

Saylani Welfare is also involved in this project of the Government of Pakistan as a partner and is also providing food through the Mobile Dastarkhuwan at the doorstep of the people.

Services - Social Welfare - Microfinance

Microfinance (Apna Karoobar)

Saylani Welfare is providing easy installment loans to small business owners. At the same time, Saylani is helping people whose businesses were destroyed in Covid – 19 by the lockdown. All these items are provided on easy monthly installments without interest and without profit.
